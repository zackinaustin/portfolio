UT-Parking




A mobile app developed for solving the problem of hard to find parking places in main campus for UT students and faculties. Make it easier in searching parking spots, finding nearby parking places and set alarm of remaining time.

Searching parking spots and filter by permits, parking type and price. Different colors and shapes stands for different parking types and availability. (Red, orange and green means low, medium and high availability)






List view & Parking information


View search results with list view to have more information such as the number of parking spots left, distance and price. Click to view more detailed information such as picture, address and phone number.
