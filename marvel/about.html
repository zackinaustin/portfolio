About Marvel Heros Database

Marvel Heros Database website is a course project for INF 385M Database Management of School of Information at UT Austin. User can explore features through our database such as search heros by group, by justice or villian, find heros in your favorite city, find heros by creators and a list view of all heros.

The database is developed using PHP, MySQL, HTML and CSS. The website design is accomplished with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and Sketch.

The team members are Mengpin Dai, Xiangkun Dai, Xinqi You and Yang Zhang.

We would like to thank our professor Stan Gunn for his dedication of teaching and guidance to our project, we appreciate all the valuable suggestions.
