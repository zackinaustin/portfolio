Libro


Libro distinguishes itself from other library mobile applications through its provision of step-by-step directions and real-time navigation to help users find books and study rooms within the physical library space.




iBeacon


Using iBeacon technology to deliver relevant information (book recommendation & information) to users based on location and also guide users to desired locations. Integrating current user needs to enhance the physical and technological interactions in the library setting.




Catalog Search


Searching for a book results in display of book images, title, author, book availability, book rating and book location. The user can choose to add the book to a list, preview the book, check out the book, or view similar books.




Study Room Reservation


Finding and reserving group study rooms by users is simplified by giving users views of currently available space, filer options to find the right study space, and details about each room.




Navigate


Navigating to the location of the book using step by step directions and real time navigation. Upon arriving at the book’s loca- tion, they can use the app to check out the book and see nearby books of interest.
